**It was a pleasure to be part of this speaker lineup at Workplace Ninjas Norway**

My session was focused on Housekeeping in Entra ID. I love this topic, because to have a secure tenant it is important to eliminate clutter and all the kind of unused objects.

When I discussed this topic at WPNinjas Norway I had new content with a bigger focus covering AI Technologies like Security Copilot, Agents in Entra and of course the powerful Microsoft MCP Server for Enterprise

Discussions after my talk showed me how interested admins are to have a clean tenant and how the appetite for cutting-edge tools to find unused stuff.

Lots of live demos and thanks to Murphy's Law staying quiet for once, every single one ran smoothly.

**Thanks to the WPNinja Norway team and everyone who attended and asked great questions.**
